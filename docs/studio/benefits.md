# Fasihi Employee Benefits & Wellness

Fasihi is committed to providing competitive, comprehensive benefits to support our team members' professional growth and personal health.

## Health & Insurance
* **Medical Cover:** Fasihi provides full medical insurance cover for employees and up to three dependents, partnered with Jubilee Insurance.
* **Dental & Optical:** Annual dental and optical allocations are included within the Jubilee package.

## Paid Time Off (PTO)
* **Vacation Days:** 21 days of paid vacation per year.
* **Sick Leave:** 10 days of paid sick leave.
* **Maternity & Paternity Leave:** 90 days of paid maternity leave and 14 days of paid paternity leave.

## Professional Development
* **Learning Budget:** Employees receive an annual stipend of $1,000 for courses, conferences, or technical books. Sarah approves professional development reimbursement requests.
