# About Fasihi

Fasihi is a premier boutique software engineering and product design firm based in Nairobi, Kenya. Our mission is to bridge the gap between complex engineering and human-centered design, building products that solve real-world problems.

## Core Beliefs
* **Design is Engineering:** We believe that code is clean when design is clear, and vice versa.
* **Small, Elite Teams:** We keep squads small and highly autonomous to optimize velocity and output.
* **Open Source & Extensibility:** We design products with API-first architectures, enabling developers to build integrations and extensions easily.

## Office Location
Our primary engineering office is located in Nairobi, Kenya, serving as a hub for software innovation in East Africa.
